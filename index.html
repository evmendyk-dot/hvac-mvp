The Process

Three steps. Confirmed in minutes. No calls required.

01

Customer submits a request

Your customer visits a shareable booking link — no login, no app download. A short form captures their name, address, problem, and phone number in under 60 seconds.

02

BookIt handles the conversation

The system texts them from your dedicated number within minutes. A simple YES reply confirms the appointment and triggers a Stripe payment link — automatically.

03

Payment collected before dispatch

A service fee is charged via Stripe before your technician leaves. Every interaction — text, stage, and payment — is logged in your live analytics dashboard.
