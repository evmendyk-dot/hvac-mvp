The Process

Three steps. Confirmed in minutes. No phone calls.

01

Customer submits a request

Your customer visits a shareable booking link — no login, no app download. A short form captures their name, address, problem, and phone number in under 60 seconds.

02

BookIt handles the conversation

The system texts them from your number within minutes. A simple YES reply confirms the appointment and triggers a Stripe payment link automatically.

03

Payment collected before dispatch

A service fee is charged via Stripe before your technician is sent. Every interaction — text, stage, and payment — is logged in your live analytics dashboard.

Zero missed after-hours calls

Customers can book at any hour. Your system responds instantly — even when your office is closed.

Payment upfront via Stripe

Service fees are collected before dispatch. No more chasing invoices or no-shows.

White-labeled to your brand

Customers interact with your company name and number. BookIt is invisible infrastructure.
